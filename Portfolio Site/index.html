Brandon Vernon

Front-End Web Developer

Previously the lead brewer and operations manager at Strange Land Brewery in Austin, Texas, I am now a front-end web developer currently employed at Engagency. I am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Git.

At Engagency I develop responsive web applications and pages for our clients. I create mockups and wireframes, manage and maintain the client’s site and content, test for quality assurance and provide documentation.

While being the lead brewer I developed and managed operations and the staff, was instrumental in growing revenues by maximizing efficiency of all operations, managed the website, formulated recipes, and worked with label designs.

My educational background is in design and art from the University of North Texas and I am proficient in basic accounting and excel modeling.

My talents in art and design combined with my mathematical and logical approach to problem solving lead me to pursue web development as a hobby. Quickly that hobby turned into a passion. This passion added to my tenacious work ethic and desire to progress has put me on my current path and has made me a valued asset to any team.
